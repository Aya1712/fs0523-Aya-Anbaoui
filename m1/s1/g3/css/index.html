Focaccia with caramelized onions,cranberries and bire

Wow your taste with this simply fantastic focaccia that will soon be your favorite appetizer of all times!

Featured it:

Fresh cranberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups lukewarm water
  • 1 cup extra vergin olive oil

For the topping

  • 3 medium onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1o ounces sliced bire
  • 1 cup of frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium sized mixing bowl, miz the dough with all the ingredients untill perfectly blended.You don't need to actually knead the dough,though sometimes I do knead it a few times in the bowl to incorporate the remaining flour...
